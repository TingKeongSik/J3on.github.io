Origin of Our Wood ?

The woods came from our company’s planting locations. We are using the trees from the planting locations and planting them back with the slogan “ One ringgit. One tree” bear in mind. For donation, please proceed to the slide below.

How do we transport our furniture?

We transport our furniture by lorries for our local customers. As for international customers, we will transport it by cargo ship to the country and after that the transportation service available at the country will transport it to our customers.

Can I request a custom-made furniture?

Yes, you can. You can fill up the form for the custom-made furniture. But you will also need to add in the designing of it, the preferred color as well as the materials. You will also receive an email for the confirmation during the operating hours. Adjustments can still be made within 7 days before the making of the furniture.

Are the details of the furniture up to scale?

Yes, the furniture is the exact size stated on the website. We made it with molds and taken the measuring of the furniture by marking them on the wood before processing. The outcome will be the same as stated in details. So rest assure.

Walk-in shopping in the time being?

Yes, but on an exception. You must complete your 2nd dose of vaccination and show your certificate before entering the shop. For those who haven’t been vaccinated or only completed their first dose is prohibited to step in to the shop.

Can the partnered charity organizations be trusted?

Yes. You can check about their recent activities as well as past events hosted by them on their websites. In addition, they are qualified by the Registry of Societies (ROS) or Jabatan Pendaftaran Pertuburan Malaysia (JPPM) and follows the rules set by the department above.

FUNDRAISING FOR OUR
REPLANTATION PROJECT

One Ringgit. One Tree.

The earth’s greenhouse effect is becoming more and more serious. Planting forests is the most effective way to slow down the greenhouse effect and the lowest cost. So our company decided to organize a team to cultivate saplings. We will transplant the successfully cultivated saplings to forests where fires have occurred to help the forests to restore their original appearance as soon as possible. We will also transplant saplings to areas with loose land to stabilize the land and reduce the occurrence of landslides. We hope that everyone can contribute a little to help slow down the greenhouse effect and restore the health of the planet.

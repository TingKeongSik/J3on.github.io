  • Who Are We?

Cosy House was established in 2021 and our first retail outlet was opened at Cyberjaya. Since then, we have grown to lead the home furnishing market in Malaysia. Our experience enables us to provide the better service during your journey with us that you can trust for your home needs. We have been inspiring and enabling people to improve their everyday lives by creating an environment that is comfortable and safe promotes wellbeing to make their dream home and workplace a reality. At Cosy House, we're not only committed to your inspiration, but also making it easy to plan and buy practical, pretty solutions. We built upon a passion to help you create a home that inspires your dreams, your home, your individuality, you. Whether you need a complete solution, a small refresh or interior planning assistance, we can help you build an atmosphere that sparks creativity, energy and joy!
